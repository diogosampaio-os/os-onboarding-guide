Common First-Week Questions

What is Agentic Systems Engineering and why does it matter for my deals?
ASE is OutSystems' new platform category announced March 31, 2026. Instead of positioning as "low-code app dev," we're now the platform that governs, secures, orchestrates, and scales enterprise AI. Every prospect you talk to is building AI agents — but most don't have a safe way to do it at scale. That's our opening. Read the ASE FAQ and watch the product sizzle video before your first customer call.
What Sales Plays should I be running in Q2?
Three plays are currently live: Banking & Financial Services (BFS), Insurance, and a new Compete play (Pro Code & AI-Native Code, live this week). A new play drops every Tuesday through May. Tag all relevant opportunities in Salesforce under Primary Sales Play. Check Seismic for the full playbooks. Use Deal Mate — it now suggests the right play automatically.
What is Revenue Launchpad and will I go through it?
Revenue Launchpad is OutSystems' field-specific onboarding bootcamp, held in Lisbon. The most recent cohort completed mid-April 2026 — every participant left with a territory POV and account entry package on Day 1. Ask Bobbie Jo Frazier when the next cohort is scheduled and whether you're enrolled. If you missed this one, all replays and materials are in the Revenue Hub Canvas in #one-revenue-team.
How does the Q2 SPIFF work for me as an AE?
The Create & Close Revenue SPIFF pays 1.5× on both quota AND commission for any new ARR opportunity created March 1–June 30, 2026 that closes by June 30. As an AE, you're eligible for any deal source. Track performance on the Q2 SPIFF Dashboard. Questions → sales.operations.team@outsystems.com.
What's the best way to handle "we'll just use AI to write code" from prospects?
This is now one of our most common objections. The answer: AI tools create a "hidden stack" of architecture and security debt. Challenge their low-upfront-cost assumption — ask "How many specialist engineers does it take to get a prototype to a secure production app?" Use the Competitive Playbook to go deeper. Proof points: Axos Bank (60% faster dev, 85% less rework), Petrobras (48 solutions in 8 months, 15× ROI), The Pokémon Company (global app for 300k fans in under one month).

Three paths: (1) Your West SA partner — for anything specific to your deal (ask Josh Cranfill for the intro). (2) #solutions_ama — SA team answers field questions here. (3) #ase-internal — for ASE-specific questions. Note: following John Ferguson's departure, SA RVPs now report to Sales VPs, which means closer alignment with your region.

ONE '26 is the company's flagship customer and prospect conference, June 2–3, 2026. It's a major deal acceleration event — bring your best accounts. The internal application deadline was April 24. Ask Bobbie Jo Frazier if late applications are still possible.

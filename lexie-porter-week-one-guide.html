Company context · The big picture

On March 31, OutSystems launched Agentic Systems Engineering (ASE) — a new product category. The pitch: enterprises don't just need AI, they need a platform that governs, orchestrates, and secures AI agents at scale. "Vibe coding" (disconnected AI prototypes) creates architecture and security debt. OutSystems, via the Enterprise Context Graph (ECG), gives agents the awareness to be safe and accurate in production.

As VP GTM Planning & Execution, your job is to ensure that the field has the systems, processes, data, and operational infrastructure to execute this strategy at scale. Kim Seabrook has been clear that the company needs to help customers understand OS's value in an agentic world — GTM excellence is how the field gets there.

Key stat for executive conversations: 94% of IT leaders worry about AI sprawl, but only 12% have a platform to manage it (State of AI Development 2026, n=1,900).
